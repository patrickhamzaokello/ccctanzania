CONNECT WITH US

If you would like to worship with us in one of our services, you can take a bus to sinza kijiweni or sinza lion and then ask for shamool hotel, and there we will be waiting for you! If you are using private transportation, simply just drive to sinza kijiweni and ask for shamool hotel and you will see sign posts.

We look forward to Worshiping with you and seeing you soon!
